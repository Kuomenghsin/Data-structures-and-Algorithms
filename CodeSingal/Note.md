# 字串格式轉換
|   | 2進位制 | 8進位制 | 10進位制 | 16進位制 |
| 2進位制 | - | bin(int(x, 8)) | bin(int(x, 10) | bin(int(x, 16)) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 8進位制 | oct(int(x, 2)) | - | oct(int(x, 10)) | oct(int(x, 16) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 10進位制 | int(x, 2) | int(x, 8) | - | int(x, 16) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 16進位制 | hex(int(x, 2)) | hex(int(x, 8)) | hex(int(x, 10)) | - |
